About

The Story of BKL: Barely Keeping Life This group started in 2023 at the beginning of college. Initially, there were 10-15 students, and everyone helped each other with notes and assignments. However, as the year progressed, the group took a new turn, and the number of members decreased to 8-9. Over time, more members left, and everyone formed their own separate groups. Now, with the changing times, the group has transformed—its name, members, and purpose have all evolved. The new name is BKL: Barely Keeping Life!!! This group isn’t just about sharing notes anymore; it’s full of backchodi, mast moments, and randirona. It’s a unique group with 6 members, each with their own skills and strengths—qualities that can’t easily be put into words. In the end, I don’t know how long this group will last, but for now, it’s holding on... barely.
